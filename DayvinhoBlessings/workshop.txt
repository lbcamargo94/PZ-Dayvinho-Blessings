description=
description=[b]Find the item[/b] — Spawns in bedrooms, living rooms, toy stores and storage. Only [b]3 copies exist per map[/b]. The first event fires [b]20 seconds[/b] after picking it up.
description=
description=Every 10 real-time minutes:
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]70%[/b] — Common Blessing (10 min duration)
description=[*] [b]20%[/b] — Random Curse (10 min duration)
description=[*] [b]5%[/b] — Legendary Blessing (1.5x stronger, 15 min)
description=[*] [b]5%[/b] — Nothing happens.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Blessings (23 fixed types + per-skill XP Boost)[/h2]
description=
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]XP Boost per Skill[/b] — Every skill in the game has its own independent blessing granting +100% / +150% XP. Each one can roll separately!
description=[*] [b]Full Belly[/b] — Gradually reduces hunger
description=[*] [b]Fresh Water[/b] — -70% / clears thirst entirely
description=[*] [b]Refreshing Rest[/b] — -30% / -50% of current fatigue
description=[*] [b]Good Mood[/b] — -50% / -75% of current unhappiness
description=[*] [b]Inner Peace[/b] — -50% / -75% of current stress
description=[*] [b]Skilled Hands[/b] — +20% / +30% Endurance
description=[*] [b]Natural Healing[/b] — Gradually reduces pain
description=[*] [b]Resilient Body[/b] — Gradually restores endurance
description=[*] [b]Blessing of Courage[/b] — Gradually reduces panic
description=[*] [b]Survival Instinct[/b] — -30% / -50% of current Panic
description=[*] [b]Garden Gift[/b] — 2 random items (3 if Legendary)
description=[*] [b]Calm Sleep[/b] — Next sleep will be more restorative
description=[*] [b]Blessed Sun[/b] — Stops the rain
description=[*] ...and more!
description=[/list]
