description=[h1]🇺🇸 Dayvinho's Blessings[/h1]
description=
description=Somewhere in this ruined world, a pocket gnome is waiting for you. Find the [b]Pocket Dayvinho[/b] and carry it in your inventory — every [b]20 real-time minutes[/b], the little guy will decide your fate.
description=
description=A blessing, a curse, or complete indifference. Dayvinho calls the shots.
description=
description=[hr][/hr]
description=
description=[h2]⚙️ How It Works[/h2]
description=
description=[b]• Find the item[/b] — Spawns in bedrooms, living rooms, toy stores, and storage areas. Only [b]3 copies exist per map[/b]. If another survivor already found one, there may be none left for you.
description=
description=[b]• Carry it[/b] — No need to equip it. Just keep it in your inventory.
description=
description=[b]• Wait for the verdict[/b] — The first event fires [b]20 seconds[/b] after picking up the item. After that, every 20 real-time minutes:
description=
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]70%[/b] — Common Blessing
description=[*] [b]20%[/b] — Random Curse
description=[*] [b]5%[/b]  — Legendary Blessing (1.5× stronger effects, 30 min duration)
description=[*] [b]5%[/b]  — Nothing happens. Dayvinho shrugs.
description=[/list]
description=
description=The timer runs on [b]real time[/b] — sleeping and fast-forwarding don't count. Persists across saves and reloads.
description=
description=[hr][/hr]
description=
description=[h2]✨ Blessings (24 types)[/h2]
